What Is Jankathon?

Jankathon is Engineering House's build-a-thon that celebrates bold ideas and fast prototypes. Teams have a 24-hour window to design and build something functional, fun, and jank.

Event Details

Date: April 18th
Location: Erdle Commons
Team Size: idk
Theme: Jank

Bring your laptops, a sense of humor, and your favorite tools. We provide snacks, basic supplies, and a space to build.
